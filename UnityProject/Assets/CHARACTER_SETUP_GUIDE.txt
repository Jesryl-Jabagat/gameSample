ECLIPSE REBORN - Quick Character Setup

TO SEE CHARACTER VISUALS:

1. In Project window, go to: Assets > Sprites > Characters
2. Drag any character sprite to Scene view
3. Available characters:
   - Auron (Golden Warrior)
   - Cira (Blue Mage) 
   - Lyra (Green Healer)
   - Milo (Brown Rogue)
   - Ronan (Red Tank)

4. To see animations, drag the animation sheets too:
   - [CharacterName]_walk_animation_sheet
   - [CharacterName]_attack_animation_sheet
   - [CharacterName]_skill_animation_sheet

5. Press Play button to test!

TIP: Right-click in Scene view and select 'Frame Selected' to center camera on character
